A thunderous wolf, childish dog
Born to an unlikely pair - a proud wolf father and a gentle dog mother - Pluffyee is a unique hybrid with a striking appearance. These distinctive markings make him stand out, especially when paired with his cool, mysterious demeanor. Yet beneath this impressive exterior lies a heart that's surprisingly childish - even more so than his brother Plufye.
